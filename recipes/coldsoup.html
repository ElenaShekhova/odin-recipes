Cold soup

This is traditional cold soup from Denmark. Normally, it's prepared using buttermilk, but our version is vegan and does not contain animal products.

  • Almond milk
  • Soy yogurt
  • Chia seeds
  • Strawberries
  • Biscuits

How to make the soup

  1. Take all ingredients from the fridge
  2. Wash and cut berries
  3. Mix milk, yogurt, and seeds
  4. Add strawberries
  5. Add biscuits
